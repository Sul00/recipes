Ingredients:
oil (groundnut oil, olive oil, vegetable oil)
maggi cube
ground pepper (red, cayenne)

Description
Take 5 table spoons of oil or what quantity you want for your food and add to a corner of your plate
Add 1 maggi cube
Add 2 teaspoons of pepper
Mix lightly or however you want to mix or don't mix at all

You are now ready to eat with a fried yam, potatoe or plantain.
